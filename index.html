Frequently asked

Will my data survive app updates?

Yes. Your projects and tracked time are stored on your device and are kept across App Store and TestFlight updates. Data is only removed if you delete the app.

How do I back up or move my data?

Open the Projects tab and tap the cloud icon to Export a backup, or Import one on another device.

Do you collect any of my data?

No. There's no account, no analytics, and no tracking — everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
